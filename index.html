Know the Mind Before the Name

There is a certain moment in life when curiosity becomes stronger than comfort. When questions begin to matter more than easy answers. I am someone who follows that curiosity. Not defined by titles, names, or labels — but by the pursuit of ideas, experiences, and understanding how the world really works. This space is not about who I am. It is about the things worth exploring — knowledge, systems, stories, and the patterns that shape our world.

What I Am Building

Right now, most of my focus is on building something in the field of agriculture. Agriculture is one of the oldest systems that sustains human civilization, yet many of its processes still rely on methods that have not evolved with modern technology. I am currently working on a startup idea that aims to integrate modern technology with agriculture to improve efficiency and sustainability.

Academic Direction

I completed higher secondary education in the science stream with Physics, Chemistry and Biology. In the coming years I aim to pursue higher studies at the Indian Veterinary Research Institute and study Veterinary Science. My long-term academic interest lies in the field of Parasitology.

Background Story

I was born in Kota and later moved to Ajmer where my family roots are connected. Later I spent around a decade in Chittorgarh where most of my childhood and schooling took place. These experiences of living in different places helped shape my perspective and curiosity about how the world works.
